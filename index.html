Hello! I am a PhD student at Université Laval, supervised by Jean-François Lalonde and Christian Gagné. My research focuses on the investigation of machine learning and deep learning in the context of computer vision. Particularly, I am working in meta-learning and representation learning in the low supervision regimes.
